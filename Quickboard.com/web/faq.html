FAQ

What is Quickboard?

Quickboard is an online marketplace that facilitates connections between freelance consultants and businesses of all sizes. Currently, we aim to provide high-performing university students who have consulting experience from student-organizations, internships, and company recruiting with an opportunity to work for themselves. On the other hand, we currently hope to provide small businesses with affordable options for all kinds of consulting, including everything from go-to-market strategies to covid reopening plans.

What sets Quickboard apart?

Staying true to our company name, we provide a platform that minimizes the time required to match, onboard, and begin a project. All you have to do is choose a consultant and sign up for a free 20 minute appointment!

I am looking to hire a consultant. How do I get started?

Here at Quickboard, our primary goal is to get you started with a consultant as soon as possible. Below are the steps you will need to take!

  1. Look through our available professionals and choose one you like.
  2. Navigate to the professional's page and schedule a free 20 minute meeting via the scheduling link.
  3. Attend the meeting. If you agree on a project and price with your consultant, fill out the "Project Kick-off Form" in the Project section of our website.
  4. Once payment (1/2 of total price) is received, Start and Finish project! (all projects and deliverables will be completed remotely until future notice).
  5. Once all final deliverables are received, fill out the "Completed Project Form" in the Project section of our website (This includes the second payment).
  6. All done!

How much does it cost?

The price per hour is listed under each consultant's page. Exact prices will be based on the scale of the deliverable and the associated time commitment. Price details can be negotiated and agreed upon anytime during the 20 minute free call. Once a price and a deliverable are agreed upon, both parties must fill out an online contract form found on our home page.

What if I am unsatisfied with the deliverables?

We know that projects might not always go the way you want them to. We understand that this can be a large inconvenience. Thus, if the deliverable completely misses the mark, don't pay the second half. It's on us. Simple as that! See our terms and conditions for more details.

I am interested in being a Quickboard consultant. How do I sign up?

We would love to have you on board. Just shoot us an email at quickboardconsult@gmail.com!
