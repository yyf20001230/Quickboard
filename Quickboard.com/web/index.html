The Quickboard Experience

We provide freelance consulting services for small to medium sized companies. The highlights of our service are listed below

Lightning fast

We aim to minimize the time that it takes for you to get on a face-to-face video call with a consultant of your choice. By optimizing the onboarding experience, we enable you to grow your business more efficiently and effectively.

Peak Value

Our platform allows you to choose the right professionals for your business. Our specialized consultants will work with you to address the weak points of your business and achieve your long term goals.

Open Access

We want to take down the barriers to consulting and make the industry’s high value services available to businesses of all shapes and sizes. We strive to feature high-quality consultants at prices that you can afford.

About Us

Our company mission is to create a platform that makes freelance consulting easy and accessible. We decided to embark on this mission after we realized how difficult it was for most small businesses to access the high value services that the consulting industry is known for. With Quickboard, we want to minimize the financial, temporal, and technological barriers to consulting so that all businesses, from bakeries to banks, have access to a dependable platform.
